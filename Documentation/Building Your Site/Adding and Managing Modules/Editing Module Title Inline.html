Editing Module Title Inline

How to edit the title of a module inline. Note: If this setting has been disabled, the title can instead be edited by a Page Editor on Administrator on the Module Settings page. See "About Module Settings" for more details.

  1. Place your mouse over a module title to access the Edit button.

  1. Click the Edit button to enable inline title editing and then edit the title as required.

  1. Click the Update button to save your changes - OR - Click the Cancel button to cancel your changes.
