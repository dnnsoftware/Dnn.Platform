Rating a Thread

How to add a rating to a thread in the Forum module. Note: Ratings may be disabled on some or all forums.

  1. Locate and open the required thread. See "Viewing any Post"
  2. In the top right-hand corner of the module, a series of Stars are displayed.

  1. Click on the star rating that you want to give. This adds your rating to all existing ratings and displays the average rating on the thread.
