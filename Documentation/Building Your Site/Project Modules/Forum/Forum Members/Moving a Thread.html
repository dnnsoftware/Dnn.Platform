Moving a Thread

How to move a thread to a new forum in the Forum module. Moderators require moderate permissions on the particular forum to move a thread. See "Setting Forum Permissions"

  1. Locate and open the required thread. See "Viewing any Post"

  1. Click the Move link beside the post. This opens the Move Thread page.
  2. At New Forum, associated with the new forum for this new thread.
  3. Optional. At Email Users of Thread Move, to notify all users participating in this thread that this thread has been moved - OR - for no email notification.
