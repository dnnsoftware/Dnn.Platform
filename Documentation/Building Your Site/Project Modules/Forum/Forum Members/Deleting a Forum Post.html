Deleting a Forum Post

How authorized users can permanently delete a post from the Forum module.

  1. Locate and open the required post. See "Viewing any Post"
  2. Click the Delete link located to the right of the post title.

  1. Optional. Complete this step to send email notification to the poster that this post has been deleted:
    1. At Email Template, select the reason why the post is being deleted.
    2. Optional. In the Emailed Response, edit the message to be emailed to the poster.
    3. At Email User, to email the poster.
  2. Click the Delete link. This displays the message "Are You Sure You Wish To Delete This Item?"
  3. Click the OK button to confirm.
